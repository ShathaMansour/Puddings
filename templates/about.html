About Pudding’s

Pudding’s began with a simple love for warmth, comfort, and small moments that turn the ordinary into something special. Inspired by Studio Ghibli’s gentle storytelling, our café was created to feel like a peaceful refuge—soft, cozy, and filled with heart.

Every cup and pastry reflects our values: kindness, creativity, and the joy found in sharing. We hope you feel at home with every visit.
